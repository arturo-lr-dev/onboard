# Onboard - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Essence

**Onboard makes AI agents enterprise-ready.**

We're professional, modern, and bold. Not playful or cutesy. We're infrastructure for serious businesses deploying AI at scale.

---

## Visual Identity

### Logo Concept

**Wordmark:** "Onboard" in a strong, geometric sans-serif  
**Symbol:** Stylized checkmark/arrow (completion + direction)  
**Style:** Clean, technical, trustworthy

### Color Palette

**Primary:**
- **Midnight Blue:** `#0A1128` - Authority, trust, enterprise-grade
- **Electric Cyan:** `#00D9FF` - Modern, tech-forward, AI-native

**Secondary:**
- **Steel Gray:** `#7A8BA0` - Professional, neutral
- **Slate:** `#1E293B` - Backgrounds, depth

**Accent:**
- **Success Green:** `#10B981` - Positive states, completion
- **Warning Amber:** `#F59E0B` - Alerts, attention

**Neutral:**
- **Pure White:** `#FFFFFF`
- **Off-Black:** `#0F172A`

### Typography

**Headings:** Inter (Bold/Semibold) - Modern, geometric, highly legible  
**Body:** Inter (Regular/Medium) - Consistent, professional  
**Code/Technical:** JetBrains Mono - Developer-friendly

---

## Voice & Tone

**Voice:**
- Direct and confident
- Technically credible
- No-BS, solution-oriented
- Slightly bold (we're building the future)

**NOT:**
- Overly playful or cute
- Salesy/hype-driven
- Apologetic or uncertain
- Generic corporate speak

**Examples:**

✅ "Stop deploying agents blindly. Start onboarding them properly."  
❌ "We're excited to help you with your AI journey!"

✅ "Enterprise-grade agent infrastructure, ready in hours."  
❌ "Seamlessly integrate your AI solutions with our platform!"

---

## Messaging Framework

### Core Positioning

**One-liner:**  
"Enterprise-grade onboarding for AI agents."

**Elevator pitch:**  
"When you hire a human, there's a structured onboarding process. When you deploy an AI agent, it's chaos. Onboard provides the infrastructure to make agents production-ready from day one: context loading, access management, compliance, and monitoring."

### Key Messages

1. **Security-first:** No more ad-hoc credential sharing
2. **Speed:** Production-ready in hours, not weeks
3. **Compliance:** Audit trails and policy enforcement built-in
4. **Scale:** Onboard 100 agents as easily as 1

### Target Personas

**Primary:**
- CTOs/VP Engineering at AI-adopting companies
- DevOps/Platform teams managing agent infrastructure
- AI consultants deploying agents for clients

**Secondary:**
- Security/Compliance officers (risk mitigation)
- Operations managers (workflow standardization)

## Design Principles

### Landing Page

**Style:** Bold, modern, slightly editorial  
**Layout:** Asymmetric grids, strong hierarchy  
**Animation:** Subtle, purposeful (GSAP for micro-interactions)  
**Imagery:** Abstract tech visuals, NOT stock photos of people in meetings

**Inspiration:**
- Linear (clean, fast, modern)
- Vercel (bold typography, dark mode)
- Stripe (clarity, trust, detail)

### UI/Product Design

**Approach:** Data-dense but organized  
**Components:** Dashboards, tables, graphs (think Datadog/Grafana but modern)  
**Interaction:** Fast, predictable, keyboard-friendly

## Dos and Don'ts

### ✅ Do
- Use strong, confident language
- Lead with the problem (chaos of agent deployment)
- Show technical depth
- Be opinionated about best practices

### ❌ Don't
- Use generic "AI transformation" buzzwords
- Overpromise or hype
- Ignore security/compliance concerns
- Talk down to technical audience
